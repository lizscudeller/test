Feeding Families

At Home Service Solutions one of our core values is giving back to the communities we are lucky enough to be apart of. That's why one of our top priorities is to feed 1000 families each year.

We have partnered with the Waterloo Regional Food Bank and the Chilliwack Foodbank to help support and give back.

Thanks again to all of our amazing customers who have contributed and made this all happen.
If you are interested in donating please let our customer service team know and we'll be happy to coordinate it.
